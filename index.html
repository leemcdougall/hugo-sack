WHO IS THIS GUY?!
Hugo Sack is one of Dulwich Hill's most quietly busy people. He studied music and sound design at UTS while simultaneously running live music events at the Marrickville Bowling Club. He performed his own original music at The Vanguard in Newtown. He launched his own festival project the moment he graduated. He is currently listed on LinkedIn as "open to work" — which is genuinely confusing because he never stops.

THE CAREER ARC 🚀
2021
Starts Music & Sound Design at UTS
University of Technology Sydney
Enrols in Australia's first degree combining music, sound and screens — film, TV, games, online. Immediately also gets a job at the Marrickville Bowling Club. Because of course he does.
2021–24
Events Coordinator at the Marrickville Bowlo 🎳
Marrickville Bowling Club · 91 Sydenham Rd · Capacity 400
Books and runs gigs at one of Sydney's best live music venues. Hockey Dad plays here. Party Dozen plays here. Donny Benét plays here. Hugo is the one making it happen — while studying full time. Builds an entire network of Inner West bands in the process. Normal student things.
May 2023
Performs at The Vanguard, Newtown 🎤
450 Records · UTS Student Record Label · Rock, Indie & Pop Night
Performs his own original music at a sold-out showcase night. Six tracks land on Apple Music and Spotify. Songs include Psychonaut and Waste Away. Very normal student music. He's on Spotify now. Hugo is on Spotify.
2024
Graduates and immediately starts Greening Out 🌿
Dulwich Hill, Sydney · No gap year detected
Receives his Bachelor of Music and Sound Design and within weeks launches Greening Out — his own independent events project. Starts as a party. Now has a website, a Hall of Fame, a photographer, a YouTube channel, and a two-year anniversary. The man does not rest.
NOW
Running Greening Out + Freelance Sound Work 🔊
Inner West Sydney · greeningout.com.au
Booking bands across Marrickville venues, doing freelance sound design and tech work, keeping the Inner West music scene alive one gig at a time. LinkedIn says open to work. His calendar disagrees.
