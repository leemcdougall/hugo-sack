Hugo Sack

Musician. Sound designer. Gig promoter. Dulwich Hill local. Builder of things. This is his story.

Who is Hugo Sack?
Hugo grew up to become one of Dulwich Hill's most quietly busy people. He studied music and sound design at UTS while simultaneously running live music events at the Marrickville Bowling Club. He performed his own original music at The Vanguard in Newtown. He launched his own festival project called Greening Out the moment he graduated. He is currently described on LinkedIn as "open to work" — which is confusing, because he never stops working.

The Career So Far
2021
Starts studying Music & Sound Design at UTS
University of Technology Sydney
Enrols in Australia's first degree combining music, sound and screens — film, TV, games, online. Immediately also gets a job at the Marrickville Bowling Club. Because of course he does.
2021–24
Events Coordinator at the Marrickville Bowlo
Marrickville Bowling Club · 91 Sydenham Rd
Books and runs gigs at one of Sydney's best live music venues. Capacity 400. Hockey Dad plays here. Party Dozen plays here. Donny Benét plays here. Hugo is the one making it happen, while studying full time. Builds an entire network of Inner West bands in the process. Normal student things.
May 2023
Performs at The Vanguard, Newtown
450 Records · UTS Student Label · Rock, Indie & Pop Night
Performs his own original music at a sold-out showcase night at The Vanguard as part of UTS's student-run record label. Six tracks land on Apple Music and Spotify. The songs are called things like Psychonaut and Waste Away. Very normal student music.
2024
Graduates and immediately starts Greening Out
Dulwich Hill, Sydney · No gap year detected
Receives his Bachelor of Music and Sound Design and within weeks launches Greening Out — his own independent events project. Starts as a party. Now has a website, a Hall of Fame, a photographer, a YouTube channel, and a two-year anniversary. The rest is history.
Now
Running Greening Out, doing freelance sound work
Inner West Sydney · greeningout.com.au
Booking bands across Marrickville venues, doing freelance sound design and tech work, keeping the Inner West music scene alive one gig at a time. LinkedIn says open to work. His events calendar disagrees.
