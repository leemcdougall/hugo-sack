ABOUT
Hugo Sack is the creator and organiser of Greening Out — a local events project started in 2024. Beginning as a party, he's still kicking on. A graduate of UTS's Bachelor of Music and Sound Design, Hugo spent his student years booking and running gigs at the Marrickville Bowling Club. He's a musician, sound tech and freelance creative based in Dulwich Hill, Sydney. We welcome any and all local creatives to come work with us.
